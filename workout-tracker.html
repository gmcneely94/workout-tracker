Stretches Optional
All stretches avoid hip flexion past 90° and internal rotation — both compress the cam lesion against the acetabulum.
Supine glute stretch (strap/towel)
2 × 30–45 sec each side
Loop strap around back of thigh, draw leg toward you keeping hip below 90°. Do NOT pull knee to chest. Stop at first hint of groin pinching.
Supine hamstring stretch (strap)
2 × 30 sec each side
Loop strap around foot, extend leg upward only as far as you can without groin pinching. Keep knee slightly soft.
Standing hip flexor stretch (lunge)
2 × 30 sec each side
Low lunge, back knee on floor. Torso upright — don't lean forward or tilt pelvis. Use yoga block for front hand support if needed.
Sidelying quad stretch
2 × 30 sec each side
Lying on side, bend top knee and hold ankle behind you. Hips stacked, pelvis neutral — don't arch low back.
Thoracic rotation
8–10 reps each side
Seated or sidelying with knees stacked, rotate upper back only. Completely hip-safe.
Legs up the wall
3–5 min
Hips close to wall, legs resting vertically. Gravity does the work — no active hip flexion.
