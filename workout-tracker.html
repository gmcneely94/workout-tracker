Stretches Optional
All stretches avoid hip flexion past 90° and internal rotation — both compress the cam lesion against the acetabulum.
Supine glute stretch (strap/towel)
2 × 30–45 sec each side
Hip below 90°. Do NOT pull knee to chest. Stop at first hint of groin pinching.
Supine hamstring stretch (strap)
2 × 30 sec each side
Leg upward, knee slightly soft, stop before groin pinching.
Standing hip flexor stretch (lunge)
2 × 30 sec each side
Low lunge, back knee on floor. Torso upright. Use yoga block for front hand if needed.
Sidelying quad stretch
2 × 30 sec each side
Top knee bent, hold ankle behind you. Hips stacked, pelvis neutral.
Thoracic rotation
8–10 reps each side
Seated or sidelying, rotate upper back only. Completely hip-safe.
Legs up the wall
3–5 min
Hips close to wall, legs resting vertically. Gravity does the work.
